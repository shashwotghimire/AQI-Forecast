Understanding AQI Levels

0 to 50

Good

Air quality is satisfactory, and air pollution poses little or no risk.

51-100

Moderate

Air quality is acceptable. However, there may be a risk for some people.

101-150

Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups

Members of sensitive groups may experience health effects.

151+

Unhealthy

Everyone may begin to experience health effects.

Features & Usage

1

Real-time Monitoring

The home page displays current AQI levels from various monitoring stations. Data is updated every hour to provide the most accurate information.

2

Forecasting

Our forecast page provides 24-hour predictions based on historical data and weather patterns. Use this to plan outdoor activities.

3

Historical Analysis

Access historical data to view trends and patterns. Filter by date range and location to analyze air quality changes over time.
